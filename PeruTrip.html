Trip to Peru

Recently, five members of our team returned from a field research Lima, Peru. There, they spent eight days partnering with MEDLIFE International. Our team members volunteered in the MEDLIFE clinics to improve their understanding of how healthcare technology is administered in developing countries. They gained deep insights into the community and clinic system and came back with many new ideas for medical devices, some of which are now in development.
